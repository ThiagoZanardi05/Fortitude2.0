FLOORING

SBR and EPDM Tiles

Designers can customise gym flooring by adjusting the concentration and colour of EPDM granules to match any interior. Fortitude Gym Flooring boasts extensive experience and a strong reputation among top UK health club operators.

The Tile is made from recycled rubber, cleaned and transformed into odourless grains (1mm to 3mm wide). These grains are bound, moulded, and coated with polyurethane, making them ideal for gym floors and lifting areas. A silicone compound is added at the end of production to keep the mats fresh during storage and transport in cold conditions.
